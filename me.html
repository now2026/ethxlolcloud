📖 ▸ A Handful of Coins – A Story Never Told ▼

The Beginning: Curiosity, Nothing More
I wasn't dreaming of wealth. No yacht in Monaco. No house on a California beach. All I had was a quiet curiosity and a handful of coins — small enough that losing them wouldn't change a thing. So I stepped in.

Scene One: The Market Never Sleeps
I found a world that doesn't know night. News every second. Charts rising and falling like a restless heartbeat.
📈 A price climbs. 📉 A price falls. 📰 News from a central bank. 🤖 An AI project. ⚽ A football club selling tokens. Everything happening at once. Everything affecting the little chart I was watching.

Scene Two: Lessons in Deception
I didn't know the world was so creative with fraud. Projects with shiny promises. Success stories written in golden letters. People crying on camera: "I lost everything."
I learned quickly: "If it sounds too good to be true, it probably isn't true."

Scene Three: Dreams You Can't Buy with Money
In a corner of those noisy platforms, I saw something else. A young man explaining his AI app to help farmers. A girl seeking funding for an educational game. A developer working alone on open source, asking for a few coins to keep his servers running. They weren't scammers. They were ordinary people with a dream — and no bank. Some failed. Some are still waiting. A very few succeeded. But all of them tried.

Scene Four: What I Actually Gained
My handful of coins didn't make me wealthy. But it bought me:

What I GainedHow I Got It
Knowledge of economicsTracking central banks & market moves
Understanding of technologyDecentralization, smart contracts, blockchains
Reading peopleRecognizing deception vs sincere dreams
Strange patienceSometimes the best move is not to move
A story to tellLike this one, which you're reading now

The End: I'm No Longer There
I left the market after a while. Not because I lost everything. Not because I won enough. But because I learned what I came to learn. Those handful of coins served their purpose. They were tuition fees — at a university that gives no diplomas, only insight.

🕊️ What Remains With Me Today
Not many coins remain. But what remains is: 🌊 Curiosity that never dies. 📚 Eyes that know how to read between the numbers. 🤍 A heart not easily fooled. ✍️ A pen writing these words. And ethx.lol is where I keep all of these things.
